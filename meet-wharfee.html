Meet wharfee

Blog 路 python docker renaming 路 2015-08-07 路 Iryna Cherniavska

Meet wharfee - previously named dockercli. It is a shell for Docker that can do autocompletion and syntax highlighting, as well as some useful shortcuts.

I had to rename my tool to avoid stepping on any official trademarks and to comply with a legal request. Wharfie, according to wikipedia, is a synonym for someone who works in the docks. And I thought it's a cute word... very important!
